Political Advertisement Disclosure

Why should organizations be required to disclose their political advertisements?
The unregulated state of our campaign finance system and the proliferation of similarly unregulated digital platforms is a dangerous combination, especially considering the well-documented existence of foreign interference in our elections. This bill requires organizations to disclose the details of their purchase. This will increase transparency in our electoral processes, and dissuade foreign actors.
